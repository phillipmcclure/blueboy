Blue Boy is a contemporary art space located in Savannah, Georgia. We are committed to exhibiting evolving art practices in the American South and beyond, connecting our community to thoughtful work and programming that excavates the through lines that run among us.

Located behind 406 E 65th Street (street parking is available, and a path leads around the house to the gallery).

We are open during exhibition receptions and other programming, as well as by appointment. We will also hold visiting hours shared weekly on Instagram.
